Art Scene Release Notes

1.0.34 9/7/2018

Added a HUD for camera help. Show and hide it by typing "h".

Hold down the control key when rotating the camera right or left causes the camera to rotate towards the cardinal directions.
1.30 9/6/2018

Fixed serious bug with the Heads Up Display.

Type "c" to hide the camera HUD. Type "c" again to show it.
1.23 9/5/2018

The status bar at the top of the window has been replaced by a HUD (heads up display).

Get Info mode is now evoked by "i" instead of command-i. Typing "i" again exits Get Info mode
1.22 9/4/2018 Somewhere the camera movement became confused. Hopefully this clears it up.
1.21 9/2/2018 Fixed a bug where a picture or image could be reduced to zero dimensions. There is now a minimum size of 2" for images and 4" for pictures.
