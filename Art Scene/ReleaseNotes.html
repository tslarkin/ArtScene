Art Scene Release Notes

1.2.1 9/13/2018

Boxes are now solid. You can't push them through walls, or through each other.

As usual, miscellaneous bug fixes and minor improvements.
1.2 9/9/2018

Boxes!

We now have boxes (rectangular prisms), which can be used to represent furniture or other objects on the floor of the gallery. Place one by right-clicking on a location on the floor and selecting Add Box from the menu. As you move the mouse over the faces, the cursor will change shape to indicate that a drag will change the box's size or position. Right-click on the box to get a menu to delete the box, change its size or position with the keyboard, or change its color.

The last release killed Get Info. It's back.

As usual, miscellaneous bug fixes and minor improvements.
1.1 9/8/2018

The right mouse click is back!

Changes to icos. The old application icon will now appear on Art Scene documents. There's a new application icon.

As usual, miscellaneous bug fixes and minor improvements.
1.0.34 9/7/2018

Added a HUD for camera help. Show and hide it by typing "h".

Hold down the control key when rotating the camera right or left causes the camera to rotate towards the cardinal directions.
1.30 9/6/2018

Fixed serious bug with the Heads Up Display.

Type "c" to hide the camera HUD. Type "c" again to show it.
1.23 9/5/2018

The status bar at the top of the window has been replaced by a HUD (heads up display).

Get Info mode is now evoked by "i" instead of command-i. Typing "i" again exits Get Info mode
1.22 9/4/2018 Somewhere the camera movement became confused. Hopefully this clears it up.
1.21 9/2/2018 Fixed a bug where a picture or image could be reduced to zero dimensions. There is now a minimum size of 2" for images and 4" for pictures.
